The Effects of Global Warming on the Artic

By Angelo Joseph Giovannoni

The Artic is a sacred biome that is known for its harsh winter climates and unique species that inhabit it. Its winter's are well known for covering the entire region with ice, creating a beautiful landscape that many would dream to visit. However, as a result of global warming and climate change, this paradise and its inhabitants are quickly dwindling away.
